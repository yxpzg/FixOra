Introducing The Company

FixOra is a startup specializing in remote industrial maintenance. We help small businesses (SMEs, TPEs, and startups) efficiently manage their machinery without the need for an in-house maintenance team. Using solutions based on CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management Systems), IoT, and predictive analysis, we reduce equipment downtime, optimize maintenance operations, lower costs, and improve machine performance.
